Logo Designs

These are the three logo designs I have commissioned in my short career as a Graphic Designer. Started with Cardinal Woodworks and the owners parents loved the new Logo so much that they asked if I could do logos for both of their companies. Which lead to a Quality Coatings rebrand and a brand new logo for new business Timeless Accents.

Cardinal Woodworks

Cardinal Woodworks is a patio furniture company based out of Apex, North Carolina. They create the pieces using refurbished wood pallets, staining them and then adding on cushions in a color of the customers choosing! The owners gave me some essentials that they wanted in the logo and I did my best to capture that for them! In the end, they were extremely pleased with how it turned out, as was I!

Quality Coatings

Quality Coatings is an interior and exterior painting company that has been around for a handful of years so I was happy to be asked to head the redesign of the logo. Since the wife of the owner liked the logo above so much, she wanted her husbands company to have the same type of design. I was given some guidelines and added my own little touch to it with the paint stroke and brush at the bottom of the circle. Once again, the customer was happy and appreciated how often I made sure we were on the right track.

Timeless Accents

Timeless Accents is an Antique company that sells chandeliers, lamps, and other furniture. The owner did not have a true logo since she was just starting out but she had some ideas for the logo that involved chandeliers, but I had something different in mind. I designed a few following her guidelines, but sent over my take on the logo that I thought of right away when I was given the name of the company. The clock was a big hit and she ended up wanting to go that way instead! I believe this one is definitely my favorite of the three because it was something I came up with which was pretty cool since I am just starting out!
